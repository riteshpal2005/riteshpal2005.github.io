Privacy Policy for LedgerLite

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LedgerLite ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

LedgerLite is designed as an "offline-first" application. Your financial data, including accounts, categories, and transactions, is stored locally on your device via an SQLite database.

If you choose to create an account to use our cloud backup features, we use Firebase Authentication. We collect your email address solely for the purpose of authenticating your account and securing your data.

2. Cloud Synchronization

If you enable cloud sync, an encrypted snapshot of your local database is stored securely using Google Cloud's Firestore infrastructure. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with any third parties. It is stored exclusively so you can restore your data on other devices.

3. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You may delete your cloud data at any time from within the app settings. Deleting your account will permanently purge your data from our cloud servers.

4. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository linked in the application.
